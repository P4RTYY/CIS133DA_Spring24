Tru Blue Tiling specializes in pool renovation, bringing older pools back to life. Additionally, we also work with new swimming pools. Starting from the concrete shell all the way to filling it up. Tru Blue Tiling is committed to creating the best possible outcome for either your renovation or new construction.

Pool Renovations:

Bringing your older pools back to life by improving the aesthetics and restoring functionality. From repairing worn out surfaces to total pool upgrades. Tru Blue Tiling offers a wide range of renovation options.

New Pools:

Tru Blue Tiling offers custom designs and expert installations aimed to satisfy any client's preference. Focusing on quality and attention to detail, we ensure that every new pool is durable, functional and most of all; a centerpiece for your backyard.
